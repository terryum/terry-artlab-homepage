I'm someone who changes everyday life through AI & Robotics Tech (ART). I care about making technology that starts in the lab actually used in people's lives. That's why I sometimes leave research to build companies, or step into bigger fields where the work matters most. Along the way, I enjoy writing down the everyday questions that come to mind.

I've researched AI and robotics at Seoul National University, University of Waterloo, and KIST, and in 2019 I founded ART Lab. Now, after being acquired by Cosmax, I'm building Physical AI here — working to make technology solve real problems in the beauty manufacturing industry.

On a personal level, I tend to hold onto questions like "how should I live?" for a long time. I cherish the small joys of being with my family, while staying committed to making the world a little better with the abilities I have.
